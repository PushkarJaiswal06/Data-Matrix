The Indian Premier League (IPL) is a professional T20 cricket league founded in 2008 by the BCCI. It features franchise teams from various Indian cities and attracts top international players. The tournament includes a round-robin format followed by playoffs.with the winning team awarded the IPL trophy. Known for its high entertainment value, the IPL combines cricket with celebrity involvement and fan engagement. It has become one of the most-watched and financially successful cricket leagues in the world.
The Big Bash League (BBL) is a professional T20 cricket league in Australia, founded in 2011. It features franchise teams representing major cities and regions, competing in a round-robin format followed by finals. The BBL attracts both Australian and international cricketers.
The Premier League is a professional football league in England, founded in 1992. It features 20 clubs from across the country competing in a round-robin format, with each team playing 38 matches per season. The league is known for its high level of competition, attracting top players and managers from around the world.
The World Chess Championship is the premier international chess competition, determining the world's best player. It has been held since 1886 and is organized by FIDE (the International Chess Federation). The championship features a match between the reigning champion and a challenger.

La Liga is the top professional football league in Spain, founded in 1929. It features 20 teams competing in a round-robin format, with each team playing 38 matches per season. The league is renowned for its high-quality football, with top clubs like FC Barcelona.
